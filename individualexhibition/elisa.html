Out of the ordinary

"Out of the Ordinary by Elisa Carabott is a captivating exploration of beauty in the mundane, transforming a traditional still life of flowers into a vibrant and dynamic composition. Combining intricate details and striking colors, the artwork blurs the boundaries between natural forms and digital interpretation, inviting viewers to find extraordinary wonder in everyday subjects. Through this piece, Carabott celebrates the resilience and complexity of nature while reflecting on the transformative power of art and technology."
