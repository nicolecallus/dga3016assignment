Roots beneath the surface

Roots beneath the surface

"Roots Beneath the Surface" by Claudia Agius is an immersive installation that explores the hidden connections between nature, heritage, and humanity. Through glowing ceramic roots, suspended wooden sculptures etched with ancestral patterns, and projected visuals of natural and human cycles, Agius invites viewers to reflect on the invisible networks that sustain life and identity. The work is a tactile and sensory meditation on the fragility and resilience of the forces that connect us to our past and to one another.
