Out of the ordinary

'Out of the Ordinary', I wanted to explore the intersection between the everyday and the extraordinary. Flowers, often dismissed as simple decorative objects, hold profound beauty and complexity when observed closely. By reinterpreting them through a mix of traditional and digital techniques, I aimed to blur the boundaries between natural and artificial, ordinary and extraordinary. The work is both a celebration of nature and a reflection on the ways technology allows us to reimagine and preserve fleeting beauty. Through vibrant colors and intricate details, I hope to invite viewers to pause and find wonder in the small, often overlooked aspects of life."
